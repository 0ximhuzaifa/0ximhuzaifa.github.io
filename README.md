## 👋 Overview

Welcome to my **Penetration Testing Knowledge Base**, a place where I document my journey in offensive security through practical writeups, technical notes, and security resources.

The purpose of this website is to organize my learning in a structured and accessible way. Instead of keeping scattered notes and Markdown files, I wanted a centralized platform where topics can be easily explored, revisited, and expanded as my knowledge continues to grow.

Each writeup is written to reflect the natural flow of a penetration test. Starting with **🔍 reconnaissance** and **📡 enumeration**, I document how individual findings lead to new discoveries, how they are validated, and how they ultimately contribute to **⚡ exploitation** and **🔑 privilege escalation**. Rather than simply presenting commands, I aim to explain the reasoning behind each step and how different pieces fit together throughout the assessment.

Although the website currently focuses on practical penetration testing writeups, it is designed to evolve into a broader knowledge base. Future content may include:

* 📝 Technical blogs
* 📖 Pentesting cheatsheets
* 🔬 Security research and technical notes
* 🎓 Certifications
* 🐞 Bug bounty writeups *(where disclosure policies permit)*

Whether you're here to explore a writeup, learn a new technique, or simply follow my progress, I hope you find something useful along the way.
